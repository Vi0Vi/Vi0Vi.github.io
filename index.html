Project

Astrophysics Interstellar Medium Fluid Mechanics Run

Growth of Hydrogen in Interstellar Medium

This project aims to investigate how the growth of dust (metals consisting of carbon, oxygen, and silicon) influences the growth of molecular hydrogen from ionic hydrogen in the interstellar medium (ISM). Molecular hydrogen is essential to the formation of early galaxies.

Key Finding & Methodology

By running fluid mechanics simulations on a supercomputer and comparing the outputs with analytical solutions, we can determine the critical role dust grain surfaces play in catalyzing molecular hydrogen synthesis.
